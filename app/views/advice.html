Having problems with your feelings or thoughts? Worried about things that are happening in your head?

Did you know that your local doctor - known as General Practitioner or GP - can help you with that?

We know it can be scary, worrying and confusing looking for help.

Doc Ready is here to help you to get ready to make a visit to the doctor.

Use our checklist to prepare a list of things you'd like to talk to a doctor about so you can take it with you.
