We know that it can be difficult to talk to people when you're having difficulties with your mental health. Doc Ready helps you get ready for the first time you visit a doctor to discuss your mental health.

  1. Info & Advice

    Doc Ready has info and advice about what to expect and how to plan speaking to a GP about your mental health.

    Take a look…

  2. Build Your Checklist

    It can be awkward, scary or embarrassing talking about your mental health. Doc Ready helps you to plan what you're going to say to your doctor. Choose from lists of things you might want to talk about or add your own.

    Get started…

  3. Be Prepared

    Doc Ready creates a checklist you can take with you so you don't get sidetracked or forget. Take your thoughts with you to your GP appointment using the export tools. It's simpler than pencil and paper
