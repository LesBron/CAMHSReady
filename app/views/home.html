Helping you to get
CAMHS Ready

CAMHSReady helps you get ready for your first visit to North Staffordshire CAMHS to discuss your emotions, behaviour or mental health. CAMHSReady has been developed by young people who have used CAMHS and staff from the service.

  1. Info & Advice

    Explains what visiting CAMHS is like and how to get the most out of your appointment.

  2. Make Your Checklist

    CAMHSReady helps you plan and remember what you want to say at CAMHS by creating a checklist that you can then take with you on your visit.
